General lifestyle saftey advice:

While the pandemic rages, life carries on. So to ensure good mental and physical health follow these tips.... Stay at home when possible. Remember to stay in regular contact with friends and family in order to avoid unwanted physcological strain due to isolation. Designate one family memeber to run erannds and cordinate obligations outside the household. The designated family member should be at low risk.
